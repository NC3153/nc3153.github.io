## Overview

Style guides are required to ensure consistency and efficiency throughout the writing process. Style guides include format guidelines, rules and best practices for the following:

- GUI Terminology
- Numbers
- Punctuation
- Graphics
- Spelling word usage
- Company word usage
- Template use
- Styles
- Formatting
