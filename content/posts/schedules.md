## Overview

The schedule complexity depends on the scope of the project and the specific deliverables involved.

### Minor Updates

Scheules for minor updates usually involve only one or two rounds of edits before routing in the Content Management System (CMS).

### Major Updates

Scheudules for major updates can involve several rounds of updates and can include the following:

- Multiple products
- Multiple product variants
- Multiple timelines based on market needs
