## Overview

Templates are required to ensure consistency, efficiency, and predictability for all deliverables.

### User Guide Templates

User guide templates include all paragraph styles, character styles, formatting, tables, page layouts, hypertext, conditional formatting, and publishing requirements needed to create, edit, and publish the final deliverables.

### Bulletin Templates

Bulletin templates are based on the user guide templates but include specific page layouts and table structures. The rest of the attributes (paragraph styles, character styles, formatting, etc.) are the same.
